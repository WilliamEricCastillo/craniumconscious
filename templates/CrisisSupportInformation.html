Crisis Support Information

If you or someone you know are experiencing a crisis or emergency there are resources availabe to help.

Options for support include the following:

  • Immediate Emergencies: Call 911 if you or someone else is in immediate danger
  • Suicide and Crisis Lifeline: Call 988 if you or someone else is in suicidal crisis or emotional distress
  • Crisis Text Line: Text HOME to 741741 to contact a trained crisis counselor
Help?In case of an emergency, please dial 911 immediately. Our crisis support page provides valuable resources for those in need, including information on immediate emergencies, the suicide and crisis lifeline, and crisis text line. We understand that seeking help can be difficult, but it is important to remember that support is available when you need it most. Whether you are experiencing a mental health crisis yourself or know someone who is, we encourage you to take advantage of the resources provided on this page and reach out for help. Remember, you are not alone.
