Crisis Support Information

If you or someone you know are experiencing a crisis or emergency there are resources availabe to help.

Options for support include the following:

  • Immediate Emergencies: Call 911 if you or someone else is in immediate danger
  • Suicide and Crisis Lifeline: Call 988 if you or someone else is in suicidal crisis or emotional distress
  • Crisis Text Line: Text HOME to 741741 to contact a trained crisis counselor
